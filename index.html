Time Valley Authority

The Time Variance Authority (TVA) is a fictional organization in the Marvel Comics Universe.
The Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA) provides electricity for 153 local power companies.
The Time Valley Authority is a time line blend of these two companies creating one fictional
power house that provides power to the multiverse while also working to keep the timeline stable.

This is a cross over timeline fictional website. Made as a creative project, I own nothing used here. Built with HTML, CSS, Flexbox and a pinch of Javascript.
